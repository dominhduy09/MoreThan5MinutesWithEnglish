Increase to: tăng đến mức nhất định
- The rate of students attending private schools increase to 25% last year 

Increase by: tăng thêm một khoảng nào đó 
- The rate of students attending private schools increase by 5% last year,
from 20% to 25%

Increase in: có một sự tăng trong một cái gì đó 
- There has been an increase in the number of students attending private 
schools
